A New Approach to Students Education

Welcome to Kabete Vetlab Secondary! We are a vibrant and inclusive learning community dedicated to providing our students with a high-quality education that prepares them for success in their future endeavors. Our school is committed to fostering a safe and supportive environment where all students feel valued and inspired to achieve their full potential. With a team of experienced and passionate educators, we strive to create engaging and innovative learning opportunities that empower our students to become lifelong learners and leaders in their communities. At our school, we believe in the power of education to transform lives and we are proud to be a part of that journey with each and every one of our students.

Science & Tech

Our school's technology program provides students with access to resources that enhance learning, prepare them for the digital age, and develop their problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

Music and Drama

Our school's music and dance programs provide students with the opportunity to explore their passion for the arts, express themselves creatively, and develop skills via a variety of classes and performances.

Games And Sports

Our school's Games and Sports program offers students of all skill levels the opportunity to express their creativity and develop valuable skills through a wide range of activities and workshops.

Healthy food

Our school's offers healthy and delicious food options for students, providing them with the energy and nutrition they need to excel in their studies and other activities.

Educational Tour

Our school's educational tour program offers students the opportunity to learn outside the classroom and gain hands-on experience through visits to museums, historical sites, and other educational destinations.
